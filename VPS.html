Living the Salesian Pastoral

Forming Young People with Values

The Salesian Pastoral helps students grow in Christian values, ethics, and social commitment, following the example of St. John Bosco.

Goals

  • Promote values such as solidarity, respect, and responsibility.
  • Encourage participation in cultural, educational, and recreational activities.
  • Strengthen spiritual life through prayers and celebrations.

Main Activities

  • Prayer and reflection meetings.
  • Social aid campaigns and volunteering.
  • Leadership and personal development workshops.
  • Religious celebrations and masses.

Participating in the Salesian Pastoral allows us to grow as people and learn to work in the community with strong values.
