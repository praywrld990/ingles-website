School Volleyball Team

Team and Activities

The 5th Grade IT Volleyball Team meets to practice, compete, and learn teamwork and discipline.

Trainings

  • Practice three times a week on the school court.
  • Warm-up exercises, technique, and coordination.
  • Simulated matches and internal tournaments.

Competitions

  • Inter-school competitions and friendly tournaments.
  • Recreational activities and school sports events.
  • Strategy and leadership workshops.

Participating in volleyball helps us stay active, improve skills, and learn teamwork in a fun and responsible way.
